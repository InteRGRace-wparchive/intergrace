Networks

To be part of InteRGrace network entails the possibility to co-organise, or invite each-other to events, discussions, and productions (written and visual academic/non-academic publications); give each other a mutual support, visibility on a local, national and international level; be partners in fund-raising (EU funded international project, International private/public fundings and awards). The dissemination of each collaborative/single-authored production, and our mutual visibility will be made possible by national/international mailing-lists, pubblicity in social networks, websites and weblogs.
Unlike members of the core-group of InteRGRace (that is Gaia Giuliani, Vincenza Perilli, Annalisa Frisina, Gabriele Proglio, Tatiana Petrovich Njegosh, Daniele Salerno, Elisa Arfini, Maria Chiara Patuelli, Sabrina Marchetti, Devi Sacchetto, Alessio Surian), the network is not directly involved in InteRGRace administrative, organisational, funding and locally based research, teaching, dissemination and outreach activities.
Every year in December, the appointment of new members of InteRGRace core-group is formally communicated through the publication of their names on InteRGrace website.

This page collects progressively any new adhesion we receive.
